Key Observations
The cost per enquiry is back at target — but for the right reasons. At $45 this week, we are essentially back at the March 2026 benchmark of $44. This isn’t just a result of low spend — it comes from a genuine increase in lead volume. 13 Google enquiries on $584 spend means the ads are converting more efficiently. The search trend is still in a gentle decline, but lead quality has held up well, which is the stronger signal.
Therapy leads continue to lead the way — and the numbers prove the direction is right. 13 of 19 people who reached out this week were looking for psychotherapy, compared to just 3 for medication management. This is a consistent pattern over the past several weeks and is a direct outcome of the campaign being steered toward therapy-focused keywords and content. The mix is exactly where it should be.
