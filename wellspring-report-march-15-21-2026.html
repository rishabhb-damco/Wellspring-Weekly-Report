Key Observations
Conversions are trending in the right direction. We moved from 11 to 10 conversions week-on-week (−9.1%) without increasing spend. The campaigns are becoming more efficient and we will continue to optimise toward this trajectory.
DSA is our strongest performer this week. We are seeing a cost per conversion of $57.55 on DSA vs. $84.68 on Search, with a CTR of 8.67% confirming the intent match is working well. We will be looking to shift more budget weight here in the coming week.
50.0% of leads scheduled — and the drop-off has structural causes. Several leads could not convert due to service or provider gaps. We cannot recover these through ad changes alone and would recommend the Wellspring team reviews capacity and service scope as a parallel action.
Psychotherapy is driving most inbound volume at 60.0%. We plan to test copy and creative assets that redirect condition-specific queries (ADHD, Anxiety, ADD) toward medication management. These conditions convert at higher value and we want to shift the inbound mix accordingly.
2 lead(s) went uncontacted after the consult call. We know the Wellspring team has a follow-up process in place — the question is how we can make it work harder together. Whether that's tightening the timing, refining the messaging, or aligning it with what we're seeing on the ad side, we'd like to explore where we can improve the re-contact rate.
