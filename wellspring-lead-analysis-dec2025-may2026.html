Key Insights & Recommendations
Data-driven observations from the consultation sheet analysis
📞
70%

Phone Calls Convert Nearly 1.5× Better

Across Feb–Apr, phone call leads scheduled at 70% vs 47% for form submissions. Every phone call that reaches a qualified lead is significantly more likely to convert than a form inquiry.

🏆
89%

March Phone Scheduling Rate — Outstanding

17 of 19 phone call leads scheduled in March 2026. This is the highest single-channel rate recorded and suggests strong intake handling. Replicating this consistency should be a priority.

📋
64%

Forms Dominate Volume, Calls Dominate Quality

64% of all leads come in via online forms but convert at only 47%. Phone calls are 31% of volume but convert at 70%. Focus should be on improving form-to-call conversion and reducing incomplete inquiries.

⚠️
23%

May Phone Call Rate Drop — Flag to Watch

Only 3 of 13 phone call leads scheduled in May so far (partial month). This is a sharp reversal from April's 72%. Could be seasonality, staff capacity, or leads calling without follow-through. Monitor closely.

📈
+28%

Overall Scheduling Rate Improved Feb→Apr

From 39% in February to 67% in April — a 28-point improvement in 2 months. Both channels improved, with phone calls showing the steeper upward trajectory. Trend is positive heading into summer.

💡

Recommendation: Prioritise Phone Intake

Given phone calls' 70% scheduling rate vs 47% for forms, consider adding a strong call-to-action on the website to encourage direct calls over form submissions. A dedicated intake line or same-day callback promise could meaningfully lift overall conversion.
