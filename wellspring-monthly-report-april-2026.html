The Story — 3 bullets, one sentence each

Keep each bullet to one honest sentence. What worked, what didn't, and what changed.

DSA was the standout campaign at $32 CPL, outperforming Generic Search by 2.6x — and CPL overall nearly halved to $50.95, the best efficiency we've recorded for this account.
Total lead volume dropped to 30 (down 14 from March) as the monthly budget was reduced by $618 — the volume decline is a direct consequence of the spend cut, not a quality issue.
Regional Search campaign launched on May 6, just outside the April window — April performance reflects only Generic Search and DSA, so May will be the first full read on all three campaigns together.
What to watch next month
May — if Regional Search adds 10+ leads without pushing blended CPL above $65, that's the signal to increase monthly budget and scale toward March-level volume at April-level efficiency.
