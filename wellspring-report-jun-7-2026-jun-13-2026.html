Recommended Actions
1
Investigate Generic Search CPC spike urgently. $8.33 avg CPC (Jun 11: $10.57/click) is the primary CPL driver. Check Smart Bidding targets, keyword match types, and search term reports for expensive queries that may be triggering irrelevant clicks.
2
Fix alternating campaign delivery. DSA and Generic never ran the same day. Check if they share a campaign budget or if an account-level daily spend cap is splitting budget between them. Both should run concurrently within their own budgets.
3
Add Medicaid negative keywords. 2 Medicaid declines this week (4 total over 2 weeks). Add “medicaid”, “state insurance”, “government insurance” as negatives to both campaigns, or update intake to surface eligibility earlier.
4
Shift budget from Generic Search toward DSA. DSA CPC $1.95 vs Generic $8.33 = 4.3× efficiency gap. Reallocating $150+/day from Generic to DSA would recover CPL toward the $44 benchmark within 1–2 weeks.
