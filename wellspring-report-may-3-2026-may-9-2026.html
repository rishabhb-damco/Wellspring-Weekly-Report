Key Observations
Scheduling rate dropped significantly — 53.8% this week vs. 81.8% last week. 6 of 13 leads did not convert. This is partly driven by lead quality issues (free consult expectations, incomplete forms) rather than capacity alone.
Google Ads lead quality is the primary concern this week. Of 3 explicit Google Ads leads: 2 went unscheduled (1 incomplete inquiry, 1 missed consultation call). Only Lead #11 via Generic Search converted. Explicit scheduling rate: 33.3%.
Two leads abandoned the inquiry form mid-way. Both were followed up on May 4 and May 6. One was a verified Google Ads lead. Form abandonment is a recurring pattern — an automated re-contact flow is needed.
Internal and referral leads are converting well. All 4 internal/referral leads that reached scheduling confirmed appointments. Psychology Today delivered 1 scheduled lead (Medication Management). Non-Google channels are high-quality.
Psychotherapy remains dominant at 69% of inbound. Medication Management dropped to 2 leads (15.4%). The ADHD/anxiety copy strategy should be accelerated to shift the service mix and relieve psychotherapy waitlist pressure.
Actions and Recommendations
1
Implement same-day callback for Google Ads form submissions. Lead #14 (Generic Search) didn't answer the consultation call. A follow-up SMS or email within 1 hour of submission would reduce missed connections.
2
Set up automated incomplete inquiry recovery sequence. 2 leads abandoned forms this week (with prior week patterns too). A triggered email or SMS 30 minutes after form start can recover these leads before they go cold.
3
Add "free consultation" landing page clarification. One lead expected a free consultation with a specific therapist (Kendra). Update ad copy and landing page to set consultation expectations upfront and reduce mismatched intent.
4
Add negative keywords for competitor and alternative therapies. One lead wanted Brain Balance first. Add "Brain Balance", "neurofeedback", and similar alternatives as negatives to reduce spend on leads redirecting to other providers.
5
Accelerate Medication Management copy strategy. Only 2 MM leads this week vs. 4 last week. Launch ADHD/ADD/Depression-specific ad groups to rebuild MM pipeline and reduce psychotherapy capacity pressure.
