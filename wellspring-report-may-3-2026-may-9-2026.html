Key Observations — Google
May 3–9 is the strongest Google Ads week on record. Cost Per Lead (CRM) dropped to $58 from $131 last week — a 56% improvement — driven by 6 Google-attributed CRM leads across all three active campaigns.
DSA CPA hit a new low at $15.97 — down from $22.52 last week. Six of 15 total conversions at less than a third of Search CPA. Protecting DSA's budget is the single highest-leverage action.
Generic Search spiked to 5 form conversions on May 6 alone (71.43% conv. rate on 7 clicks). Total Search conv was 6 for the week. Investigate whether this was a bid change, auction anomaly, or sustained improvement.
Regional Search launched mid-week with a $69.10 CPA — only 1 conversion on 11 clicks across 3 days. Too early to evaluate; needs 2–3 full weeks of data before budget decisions.

Key Observations
Scheduling rate dropped to 53.8% from 81.8% last week. The decline is concentrated in Google-attributed leads (2 of 6 scheduled, 33.3%) — other sources scheduled 5 of 7 leads (71.4%).
Both incomplete inquiries were from Google-attributed leads. Lead #2 (explicit Google Ads) and Lead #3 (implied) both abandoned forms on May 3. Follow-up attempts were made May 4 and May 6 — response rate unknown.
Internal referrals are high-quality this week. 3 leads from patient referrals/internal contacts — all 3 scheduled. This channel requires no spend and converts reliably.
Psychotherapy demand is growing (69%) but capacity may be limiting conversion. One lead (child) was redirected — may reflect therapist availability. Medication Management dropped to 2 leads vs. 4 last week.
Actions and Recommendations
1
Same-day callback for Google Ads leads. Lead #14 (Generic Search) missed the consultation call. Trigger an SMS within 60 minutes of form submission — this is the single highest-ROI change available.
2
Automated re-contact for incomplete inquiries. 2 form abandonments this week. Set up a 30-minute SMS trigger after partial form starts. Existing follow-up (May 4, May 6) was manual — automate it.
3
Add "free consultation" expectation-setting copy. Lead #4 wanted a free consult. Update ad copy and landing page: "Book a paid consultation call" instead of generic "Book now" to filter intent.
4
Add Brain Balance / neurofeedback as negative keywords. One parent was redirecting their child there first. These searches signal intent toward a competing service — exclude them to save budget.
5
Protect DSA's budget — it delivered a record $15.97 CPA this week. Generic Search improved to $23 CPA, but DSA remains 31% more efficient. Regional Search ($69.10 CPA, new) needs 2–3 more weeks before any budget reallocation.
