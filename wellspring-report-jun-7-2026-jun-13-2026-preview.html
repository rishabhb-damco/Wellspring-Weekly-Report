What to focus on this week
1
Investigate why the search ad costs so much per click. The Generic Search ad usually costs ~$4 per click but hit $8.33 this week. Damco will review what changed and fix it. This is the main reason our cost per enquiry is above target.
2
Check if both ad types can run on the same days. This week, only one ad ran at a time (they alternated days). If both ran together, we’d likely get significantly more enquiries for the same weekly spend.
3
Consider filtering Medicaid from our ads. Medicaid patients have enquired 4 times in the last 2 weeks, but Wellspring can’t accept them. A simple ad adjustment could stop these calls and improve our booking rate.
4
Shift more budget to the Dynamic ad. It costs only $1.95 per click vs $8.33 for Generic Search this week. Moving $100+/day toward it should bring the cost per enquiry back toward $44.
